Looking for your next Star Wars Destiny team? Can't decide with so many combinations? Looking for the perfect fit for that one character?

Let the SWD Team Builder app help!

If you are interested in the beta, please fill out this form. iOS only for now, Android will probably come at some point.

  • View all possible teams (over 2400!)
  • Select one or two-dice characters to match compatible teams
  • Set the minimum values for dice, health, and/or points
  • Exclude mixed damage teams
  • Many more features to come...
