Looking for your next SWD team? Feeling overwhelmed by all the possible combinations? Looking for the perfect fit for that one character?

Let the SWD Team Builder app help!

  • View all possible teams (over 500,000 as of CONV and AoN!)
  • Choose a format: Infinite, Standard, or Trilogy
  • Select one or two-dice characters to show compatible teams
  • Filter characters and teams by number of characters, number of dice, health, points, affiliation, faction, damage type, and set
  • Show only teams that support a plot with points and/or faction

All this for FREE!
